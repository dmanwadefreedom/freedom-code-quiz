Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

This policy explains how Dylan Ewing ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit thedylanewing.com, take the Freedom Code quiz, subscribe to emails, or purchase products.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Quiz responses: Your answers to the Freedom Code quiz, including your first name and email address
  • Purchase information: Name, email, and payment details when you buy Freedom Code or other products (processed by Stripe)
  • Email interactions: Opens, clicks, and replies to our email communications

We also collect standard analytics data automatically:

  • Browser type, device, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Referring URL
  • IP address (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Deliver your quiz results and pattern type
  • Send your email sequence (pattern delivery, nurture emails, and daily program emails if purchased)
  • Process purchases through Stripe
  • Improve the quiz, course, and website experience
  • Send occasional updates about new content (you can unsubscribe anytime)

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

  • GoHighLevel: CRM and email delivery. Your contact info and quiz results are stored here.
  • Stripe: Payment processing. We never see or store your full credit card number.
  • GitHub Pages: Website hosting.
  • Google Fonts: Typography. Google may collect standard usage data.

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This site uses minimal cookies:

  • GoHighLevel tracking code for conversion measurement
  • No advertising cookies or retargeting pixels

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Email Communications

When you take the quiz or purchase Freedom Code, you'll receive emails based on your actions:

  • Quiz takers: Pattern result email, followed by a nurture sequence (6 emails over 10 days)
  • Buyers: Welcome email, followed by 30 daily program emails

Every email includes an unsubscribe link. Click it and you're out. No hoops.

6. Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption on all pages
  • Stripe PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Access controls on CRM and admin tools

No system is 100% secure. We take reasonable precautions but can't guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access the data we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data from our systems
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time

To make any of these requests, email dylan@thedylanewing.com.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at anyone under 13. We don't knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information, contact us and we'll remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services change. The effective date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions? Email dylan@thedylanewing.com.

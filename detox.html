01What's in you right now

You are more contaminated than you think

Studies on the CDC's own biomonitoring database show the average American body tests positive for 5 or more heavy metals. Aluminum. Lead. Mercury. Cadmium. Arsenic. Barium.

Where it's coming from:

  • Water: aluminum sulfate in municipal supply, fluoride, lead from old pipes
  • Food: rice (arsenic), tuna (mercury), conventional produce (pesticides + cadmium)
  • Air: atmospheric aluminum, barium, strontium (look up the geoengineering patents)
  • Cookware: non-stick pans, aluminum foil, old coated pots
  • Personal care: deodorant (aluminum), toothpaste (fluoride), sunscreen (oxybenzone)
  • Dental: silver amalgam fillings leak mercury for decades
  • Plastics: microplastics now documented in blood, brain, testicles, placenta

What it does once it's in you: brain fog, chronic fatigue, hormone crash, skin issues, autoimmune flare-ups, infertility, memory loss, depression.

Fact: doctors wont run a heavy metal panel unless you specifically ask. Standard blood tests miss metals stored in tissue. You need a provoked urine test to see whats actually there.
02How detox actually works

Three phases. Skip one and you re-toxify.

This is where it goes wrong for a lot of folks. They take one supplement, feel worse, quit. Its because the metals get mobilized but not bound. They float around and redeposit in another organ instead of leaving.

Phase 1
Mobilize
Pull metals out of where theyre stored. Bones, brain, fat tissue. Cilantro crosses the blood-brain barrier. Chlorophyll pulls from blood.
Phase 2
Bind
Once metals are loose, they MUST be grabbed before they redeposit. Zeolite traps them in its cage structure. Chlorella and spirulina bind them in the gut so they can't be reabsorbed.
Phase 3
Eliminate
Push everything out. Sweat (sauna). Poop (fiber + water). Pee (hydration). Miss this and your body just recirculates what you mobilized.
The stack below covers all three phases. Don't skip the binder. Don't skip the sauna.
